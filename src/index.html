Can Dog Parks be Bad for Dogs?

A recent New York Times article prompted us to visit Ohlone Dog Park in Berkeley, the first of its kind in the world, and ask: Can dog parks be bad?

Dog park has become the fastest-growing type of park in the U.S.

For urban dogs, it could be the only place to get some exercise.

But sometimes, scuffles between dogs can lead to injuries.

Berkeley vet Jeffrey Freeman says it is up to the dog owners to weigh the benefits and risks.
